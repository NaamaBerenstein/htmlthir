Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

1 cup brewed coffee, chilled
1/2 cup milk or cream
1-2 tbsp sugar (optional)
Ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

Brew your coffee as you normally would and let it cool down in the refrigerator or freezer until it's cold.
In a glass, mix together the chilled coffee, milk or cream, and sugar (if using) until well combined.
Add ice cubes to the glass. Top with whipped cream, if desired. Serve and enjoy!

Note: You can customize this recipe to your liking by adjusting the amount of coffee, milk or cream, and sugar used.
You can also add flavored syrups, chocolate sauce, or other toppings if you like.

Iced Vanilla Latte

Ingredients:

1 cup brewed coffee, chilled
1/2 cup milk or cream
2 tbsp vanilla syrup
Ice cubes

Instructions:

Brew your coffee as you normally would and let it cool down in the refrigerator or freezer until it's cold.
In a glass, mix together the chilled coffee, milk or cream, and vanilla syrup until well combined.
Add ice cubes to the glass. Stir the mixture until the ice cubes are fully incorporated and the drink is well chilled.
Serve and enjoy!

Note: You can adjust the amount of vanilla syrup used depending on how sweet you like your coffee. You can also experiment with different flavors of syrup,
such as caramel or hazelnut, to create your own custom latte recipe.

Cappuccino Mix

Ingredients:

1 cup instant coffee powder
2 cups powdered milk
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)

Instructions:

In a large bowl, combine the instant coffee powder, powdered milk, cocoa powder, sugar, and ground cinnamon (if using).
Use a whisk or a fork to mix the ingredients until they are well combined.
Transfer the mixture to an airtight container for storage.
To make a cup of cappuccino, add 2-3 tablespoons of the mix to a cup of hot water or milk, and stir until the mixture is fully dissolved.
Top with whipped cream or cinnamon, if desired.
Serve and enjoy!

Note: You can adjust the amount of sugar and cocoa powder used in the recipe to your liking. You can also experiment with different flavors,
such as adding a pinch of nutmeg or cardamom to the mixture.

Irish Cream

Ingredients:

1 cup heavy cream
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz)
1 2/3 cups Irish whiskey
1 tsp instant coffee granules
1 tsp cocoa powder
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract (optional)

Instructions:

In a blender, combine the heavy cream, sweetened condensed milk, Irish whiskey, instant coffee granules,
cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and almond extract (if using).
Blend the mixture until it is smooth and well combined.
Transfer the mixture to an airtight container and store it in the refrigerator until you're ready to serve.
To serve, pour the Irish cream into glasses filled with ice, and enjoy!

Note: This Irish Cream recipe can be customized to your liking. If you prefer a stronger whiskey flavor, you can add more whiskey.
If you prefer a sweeter or more chocolatey flavor, you can adjust the amount of sweetened condensed milk and cocoa powder used in the recipe.
You can also experiment with different types of extracts to create your own unique flavor profile.
